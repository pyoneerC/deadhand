### The Self-Custody Paradox

The entire value proposition of Bitcoin is sovereignty. "Be your own bank." No intermediaries. No counterparty risk.

But sovereignty creates a succession problem.

When your client holds their own keys, they become a single point of failure. The moment they stop breathing, their family's access to generational wealth depends on:

- A piece of paper in a fireproof safe (that they may have moved)
- A password manager (that requires a master password no one else knows)
- A lawyer (who doesn't understand the difference between a seed phrase and a private key)

I've seen families lose millions because Dad was "security conscious" and told no one.

---

### What Traditional Solutions Get Wrong

**"Just tell your spouse the password."**

This fails 90% of the time. Relationships change. Memories fade. Papers get thrown away during moves. And giving someone full access while you're alive creates its own risks.

**"Put it in your will."**

Wills are public documents. Filing a 24-word seed phrase with a probate court is like publishing your bank account credentials in the newspaper.

**"Use a multi-sig with your attorney."**

Now you're trusting a third party with signing authority over your client's assets. That's the exact counterparty risk crypto was designed to eliminate.

---

### The Solution: Trustless Inheritance via Shamir's Secret Sharing

At Deadhand Protocol, we solved this with math.

We split your client's seed phrase into 3 encrypted shards using **Shamir's Secret Sharing** (the same algorithm that protects nuclear launch codes).

Here's how it works:

- **Shard A**: Client keeps (in password manager or safe)
- **Shard B**: Beneficiary keeps (printed PDF, no digital exposure)
- **Shard C**: We hold encrypted (released only when triggered)

**Any 2 shards = full recovery. Any 1 shard = mathematically useless.**

Even if we're hacked, attackers get nothing useful. Even if the client is hacked, thieves get nothing useful. The threshold is the protection.

---

### The Dead Man's Switch

Every 30 days, we send your client a simple check-in email.

Click the link = timer resets.

No lawyers. No probate. No courts. Just code.

The beneficiary combines Shard B (which they already have) with Shard C (which they just received), and recovers the seed phrase. They import it into any standard wallet and take custody of the assets.

**Time from trigger to access: 5 minutes.**
