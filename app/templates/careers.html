Careers at Deadhand

Content & Communications Lead

Remote (Global) Full-Time Operations

We are looking for a Content & Comms Producer to be the operational heart of our content strategy. You will be the internal journalist, content producer, and master storyteller who transforms raw insights into compelling narratives.

This role demands a blend of journalistic curiosity, creative storytelling, and rigorous project management. You are crafting the very stories that will inspire and attract the next generation of privacy-conscious users.

About the Role

Deadhand is the trustless standard for digital inheritance. We are a small, high-agency team building tools that protect billions in generational wealth. We don't do fluff. We don't do corporate speak. We build sovereign tools for sovereign individuals.

As Content & Comms Lead, you will own our voice. You will translate complex cryptographic concepts into human stories. You will manage our production pipeline from initial concept to final publication across all platforms.

Key Responsibilities

  • Own the Story: Personally conduct interviews with founders, developers, and users to uncover the human stories behind the technology.
  • Craft the Narrative: Write creative briefs and define story arcs for our articles and guides, ensuring every piece captures the "human closeness" that defines our brand.
  • Produce Content: Manage the end-to-end production of our content—coordinating topics, overseeing external editors, and ensuring a consistent flow of high-quality pieces.
  • Be the Quality Gatekeeper: Act as the final editor and arbiter of quality. You will personally refine articles to ensure they are sharp, insightful, and perfectly on-brand (Swiss Institutional).
  • Manage the Pipeline: Run the entire content production process like a seamless workflow, automating where possible for scale.

You’re a good fit if you...

  • Are a Natural Storyteller: You have a proven track record of finding the human angle in complex topics (e.g., tech journalism, crypto research).
  • Possess Exceptional Writing Skills: You are a world-class editor who can take a good draft (human or AI) and make it truly great.
  • Are a Systems-Builder: You thrive on creating efficient, repeatable processes.
  • Have a Keen Eye for Quality: You are comfortable being the final decision-maker on what meets the Deadhand standard.
  • Are Intellectually Curious: You are genuinely fascinated by cryptography, sovereignty, and game theory.
  • Possess Exceptional Self-Discipline: You excel in a remote environment, working autonomously to drive high output.

Why Apply?

  • Build with the best: Collaborate with top engineering and entrepreneurial minds in the privacy space.
  • Shape a community: Your stories influence who joins and what future gets built.
  • Unbounded impact: Own and scale our core content assets from Day 1.
  • True autonomy: Work remotely with freedom, responsibility, and trust.
