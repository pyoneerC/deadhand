why calculate bus factor?

in software engineering, the "bus factor" is the minimum number of team members that have to disappear (e.g., hit by a bus) before a project stalls due to lack of knowledge.

for 95% of crypto holders, the bus factor is 1.

if that 1 person is you, your family's inheritance is effectively zero. fixing this requires increasing your bus factor to at least 2 without trusting a third party.
